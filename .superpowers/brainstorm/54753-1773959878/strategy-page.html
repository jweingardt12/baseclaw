What should the Strategy page include?

Pick the features that matter most for your in-season strategy. All have working API endpoints.

1

Trade Finder

Identifies trade partners based on positional strengths/weaknesses across the league. Shows which teams have surplus where you're weak, and vice versa. Uses /trade-finder and /positional-ranks.

2

Trade Evaluator

Grade proposed trades before making them. Input "give" and "get" players, see z-score impact, category simulation, and a letter grade. Uses /trade-eval and /category-simulate.

3

Category Tracker

Track your performance in each scoring category vs league. Shows trends, targets, and which categories to punt or push. Uses /category-check, /category-trends, /punt-advisor.

4

Playoff Planner

Playoff scenario analysis — schedule strength, optimal roster moves for the stretch run. Uses /playoff-planner, /season-pace, /schedule-analysis.

Click multiple to select all that you want. These will become tabs on the Strategy page.
