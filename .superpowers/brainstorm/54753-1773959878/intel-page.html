What should the Intel page include?

These are research/information features. Select all that you want as tabs on the Intel page.

1

News Feed

Player news headlines with sentiment analysis, source attribution, and filtering by your roster players. Uses /news, /news/feed, /news/player.

2

Streaming Pitchers

Weekly SP streaming recommendations with matchup ratings, team game counts, and z-score projections. Uses /streaming, /probable-pitchers, /pitcher-matchup.

3

Waiver Wire Lab

Smart waiver analysis: weak category identification, targeted pickup recs, FAAB bid guidance, IL stash advisor, and category-simulate before adding. Uses /waiver-analyze, /faab-recommend, /il-stash-advisor, /category-simulate.

4

Closer Monitor + Probable Pitchers

Track closer situations across MLB — who's safe, who's losing the job, and handcuff opportunities. Plus daily probable starters with matchup quality. Uses /closer-monitor, /probable-pitchers.
